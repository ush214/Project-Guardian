Part C: Environmental Sampling

Testing for hydrocarbon contamination.

‚ñº

Sampling provides evidence of active or historical leaks. Maintain strict chain of custody.

  • Water Samples: Collect upstream and downstream relative to current.
  • Sediment Samples: Beside the hull and a control site at least 100 m away.
  • Analysis: Test for TPH and PAHs.
